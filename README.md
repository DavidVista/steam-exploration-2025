# Steam Dataset 2025 | Study of Emerging Developers

## About the Project

In this work, I present my analysis of the following business objective: *what are the features of game developers that result in future partnership with top publishers?* In other words, the study aims to **find** promising emerging developers and **interpret the choice**.

The project is performed according to the [CRISP-DM methodology](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/spss-modeler/saas?topic=dm-crisp-help-overview). The reports are organized in the [report](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/report) folder as follows:
- [1_business_understanding](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/report/1_business_understanding.pdf): formulation of business questions and data mining goals.
- [2_data_understanding](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/report/2_data_understanding.pdf): EDA of Steam dataset.
- [3_data_preparation](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/report/3_data_preparation.pdf): Data cleaning and preprocessing.
- [4_modeling](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/report/4_modeling.pdf): ML modeling.
- [5_evaluation](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/report/5_evaluation.pdf): Assessment of business success criteria.

The reports are supported with the experiments data from Jupyter notebooks: [EDA](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/notebooks/1_data_understanding.ipynb), [data preparation](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/notebooks/2_data_preparation.ipynb), [modeling](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/notebooks/3_modeling.ipynb), [evaluation](https://github.com/DavidVista/steam-exploration-2025/notebooks/4_evaluation.ipynb).

## Main Results

During the evaluation phase, the following two profiles of emerging developers are highly associated with future partnership with top publishers:
1. Developers with frequent discounts;
2. Developers with premium pricing, strong recent player engagement, and broad language support.
